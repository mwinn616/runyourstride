Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 No Data Collected

Run Your Stride does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. The app is a pure calculator that runs entirely on your device.

📵 No Internet Required

Run Your Stride requires no internet connection to function. No data is sent to any server, third party, or external service.

🚫 No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. There are no user accounts, no login, and no usage data recorded.

📦 What Stays on Your Device

Any values you enter into Run Your Stride (distance, time, pace) exist only in the app's memory while it is open. Nothing is saved to your device storage or transmitted anywhere.
